How It Started

Billy Mac's was founded by Billy MacDonald, a self-proclaimed gamer and foodie. Growing up, Billy spent countless hours in arcades, competing with friends and family in high-score battles. As he grew older, his love for gaming never faded, but he began to notice a void in the dining scene â€“ a place where people could enjoy delicious food and immersive gaming experiences under one roof.

In 2010, Billy turned his vision into a reality, opening the first Billy Mac's location in a small town. The restaurant quickly gained popularity among locals and gamers alike, thanks to its unique blend of tasty food, craft beers, and classic arcade games.

Over the years, Billy Mac's has expanded to multiple locations, but its core mission remains the same: to provide a welcoming space for people to come together, enjoy great food and drinks, and indulge in their passion for gaming.

Today, Billy Mac's is a beloved destination for gamers, foodies, and families alike. Whether you're a retro gaming enthusiast or just looking for a fun night out, we invite you to join our community and experience the ultimate gaming and dining destination.
